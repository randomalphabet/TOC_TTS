ToC-TTS : Single Fine-Grained Conditioning to Text-to-Speech Based on Dialogue Text Context

Junbum Kim1, and Joon-Hyuk Chang2

Department of Electronic Engineering, Hanyang University, Seoul, Republic of Korea 1, 2
johnny960423@gmail.com1,    jchang@hanyang.ac.kr 2
Anonymous submission to Interspeech 2025

Abstract


While human-computer interaction becomes more important, synthesizing human-like speech remains as a challenging issue. Conversational text-to-speech needs further research to generate natural dialogues for realistic situations. Previous research focused on understanding the conversation itself, without considering speaker relationships or emotional states. These models use pretrained models to extract feature vectors from text and audio, which causes mismatch between the ground truth and the synthesized speech. To address these problems, we propose a ToC-TTS model with two modules. First, text-only dialogue context encoder captures dialogue flow and speaker relationships using text, and generates fine-grained condition. Second, target prosody extractor adjusts by extracting prosody from ground truth speech, allowing the encoder to predict prosody. Our model achieved an mean opinion score of 3.63, demonstrating its naturalness and quality in synthesizing dialogue-based speech.

1.2. Key Methods

  • To solve the mismatch problem between the ground truth and synthesized speech, the proposed method extracts the dialogue context using only text.
  • To solve lack of information on single-modality, we made a target by using ground truth speech.
  • To make the conversation more similar to a real human interaction, a module is structured by separating the global and speaker modules.

3. Evaluation Results

    Mean Opinion Score(MOS) and Dialogue Mean Opinion Score (D-MOS) was rated from 1 to 5. And average all the score with 95% confidence rate.

    Log-F0 Root Mean Square Error (F0 RMSE) is the pitch accuracy. Pitch was extracted by pyworld library, and calculated into log scale. Then calculate with root mean square between ground truth and synthesized speech.

    Mel Ceptrial Distortion (MCD) calculate the difference between ground truth audio and synthesized speech.
